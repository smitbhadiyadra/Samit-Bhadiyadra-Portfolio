How long does it take to complete a web design project?

The time required to complete a web design project depends on its complexity and the scope of work. Simple landing page websites may take a few weeks, while larger and more intricate projects could take several months. During our initial consultation, I will provide you with a realistic timeline based on your specific project requirements. So click that contact button.

What is the typical process of working together on a web design project?

The web design process typically involves several key stages:
Initial Consultation: We discuss your project goals, requirements, and target audience.
Planning and Proposal: I create a detailed proposal outlining the project scope, timeline, and cost.
Design Phase: I develop mock-ups and wireframes to visualize the website's layout and design.
Development: Once the design is approved, I proceed to build the functional website.
Review and Testing: You have the opportunity to review the website and provide feedback.
Launch: After final revisions and testing, the website goes live.

Can you update or redesign an existing website?

Absolutely! I offer website redesign services to refresh the look and feel of your existing site or make it more functional and user-friendly. Whether you need a complete overhaul or minor updates, I can work with your existing platform or migrate to a new one if necessary.
